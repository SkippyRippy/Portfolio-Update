Amanda Johnson

I've spent a large portion of my life doing traditional art and learning all that I could about how things functioned the way that they do. Once I was older I obtained a deeper understanding of art and its place in the world. I soon realized that I wasn't meant to me a fine arts artist. I never wanted to invest all of my time into one medium. So I learned more and more subjects which eventually lead me to coding.
